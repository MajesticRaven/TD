Your goal: To protect the castle from monsters.
Your remedies: You have the ability to build 4 types of towers. When you press the left mouse button - will be built one type of towers, provided that there is enough money. When you right-click a mouse, the second type will be built.
You have the opportunity to improve the tower to 10 level with the left mouse button (you need to click on the tower). Each improvement costs (tower level * 10) and adds 10 damage per level.
Your castle has 10 health. If you do not cope with the protection and the monsters broke through to the castle, then you have a chance to win if there are 10 or less monsters for the entire level.
In the game there are 10 levels, 4 types of towers, 21 type of monsters.
Good luck!
